# Vision Attractor Overview

Vision Attractor / LOGOS v3.0 is a BI dashboard for exploring life attractors, scenario behavior, and relationships between attractor categories.

The product has two complementary modes:

- **Scenarios**: profile-driven exploration of life situations and behavior strategies.
- **Graph**: system-level exploration of attractor hierarchy, reinforcing links, conflicting links, and structural tension.

The graph is not just a visual copy of a table. Its purpose is to make relationships legible: which L2 attractors are highly connected, which nearby attractors reinforce a selected node, where conflicts concentrate, and which situations express the selected L2.

## Current Direction

The first analytical slice is focused on selected L2 nodes:

- role in the graph by weighted degree;
- counts of reinforcing and conflicting links;
- strongest nearby L2 neighbors;
- linked situations and whether behavioral markup exists.
